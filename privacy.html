Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TikTok Live Studio Draft Uploader ("the App") handles information when you use the App.

1. Information we process

The App is designed to work primarily on the user's local machine. Depending on the features you use, the App may process:

  • basic TikTok account information returned by TikTok after authorization, such as open ID, display name, avatar URL, or profile link;
  • TikTok OAuth tokens required to maintain the authorized session;
  • local configuration values such as client key, redirect URI, token file path, and selected scopes;
  • local file paths and metadata for user-selected processed video files; and
  • draft upload status information returned by TikTok.

2. How we use this information

The App uses this information only to:

  • let a user connect their own TikTok account through Login Kit;
  • display basic creator information in the local UI;
  • initialize and transfer a user-selected local video file to TikTok Drafts using the Content Posting API;
  • show upload and status logs in the local UI; and
  • maintain local session continuity for the authorized user.

3. Local storage

OAuth tokens and app configuration may be stored locally on the device where the App runs. The App does not require a centralized backend service to function for this flow.

4. Sharing of information

The App shares information with TikTok only as necessary to complete the authorization and draft upload workflow requested by the user. The App does not sell personal data.

5. Data retention

Locally stored configuration data, logs, and OAuth tokens remain on the user's machine until deleted, replaced, or disconnected by the user.

6. User control

Users can disconnect the TikTok account in the App, remove locally stored token files, and delete local log or configuration files at any time.

7. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep the App's local workflow limited to the data necessary for the integration, but no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users are responsible for securing the device where the App runs.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children. Users must comply with TikTok rules and all applicable law when using the App.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
